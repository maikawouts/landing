What is the Youngs Festival?

Step into the vibrant world of young creativity at the Youngs Festival, where the artistic spirit of today's youth takes center stage. From the captivating allure of cinema to the evocative strokes of painting, the innovative flair of fashion to the soul-stirring images of photography, and the intricate craftsmanship of pottery, immerse yourself in a kaleidoscope of artistic expressions. Across three distinct locales in Paris, Biarritz, and Lille, each venue offers a unique glimpse into the boundless talents of emerging artists. Join us on this exhilarating journey and be part of a celebration that ignites inspiration and cultivates the next generation of artistic visionaries.

Why did I create this event?

I created this event because I've always had a passion for bringing people together. I've always witnessed the power of human connection and wanted to create a platform where individuals can gather in an inspiring setting. Furthermore, my love for art played a crucial role in this initiative. I firmly believe that art has the power to transcend boundaries and spark meaningful conversations. By bringing people together around art, I aim to create enriching and memorable experiences that celebrate diversity and creative expression. This event is more than just an exhibition; it's a celebration of life, creativity, and human connection.
